About Illugo

We don't just
draw. We craft.

The name Illugo is rooted in the Korean 일고 (Il-go) — meaning "Lofty Work." Our first studio. Our first commitment. To illustration that moves forward.

Our small but highly skilled team specializes in manga and manhwa-style illustration, bringing expressive characters, dynamic panels, and polished storytelling to every project we take on. From storyboarding and art direction to inking and professional coloring, we handle the full pipeline — adapting to your story's visual language so every panel feels exactly as you imagined it.

"We don't just deliver files. We deliver work you're proud to publish."
